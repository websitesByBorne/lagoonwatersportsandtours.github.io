Luminous Lagoon

Located outside Montego Bay in a small town called Falmouth, a phenomenon occurs where microorganisms called dinoflagellates emit blue luminous light when activated. Experience the bioluminescence that exists only in certain parts of the world. This is a fun night boat tour, where you are welcome to jump in and splash around to see it up close or you can watch from the comfort of the boat. Grab a drink at the local bar before and after your tour. Restrooms available on site.

Bamboo Rafting

Rafting on the Martha Brae River is one of the island’s favorite attractions. Rafter’s Village offers a beautiful recreational facility which includes picnic grounds, a full service bar, two souvenir shops, swimming pool and restrooms. Be sure to bring cash for drinks or souvenirs on the way down the river.

Bob Marley's 9 Mile

Come experience one of the most interesting tours of Jamaica and explore Bob Marley's birthplace and resting place. Enjoy a breathtaking drive through the countryside on a journey through some of the most beautiful scenery on the island. Enjoy a drink at the bar, food, live music and dancing. A souvenir shop is also on site for those die-hard fans. Bob Marley's 9 Mile Village is a great place to experience one of Jamaica's National Treasures. Appreciate his many accomplishments during his musical career where many awards adorn the walls. Be sure to bring cash for tipping the tour guide or roadside food and drinks along the way.

Rick's Cafe - Negril

Come see why Rick's Cafe in Negril is one place you have to visit once in your lifetime! Take a road trip to Negril and see the beautiful cliffs and seasides. Visit Rick's Cafe for food, drinks, music, souvenirs and fun while watching dare devils dive and jump off the cliffs. We don't advise jumping off the cliffs, but it's fun to watch.
